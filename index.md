# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 20, 2026**

## Overview

This privacy policy describes how Jared Turner ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our applications and tools that connect to Google API Services and other third-party services.

## Information We Collect

Our applications may request access to the following types of data through Google API Services:

- **Email data** (Gmail API) — To read, send, and manage email on your behalf
- **Calendar data** (Google Calendar API) — To view, create, and manage calendar events
- **Drive and document data** (Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides APIs) — To create, read, edit, and organize files and documents
- **Contact data** (People API, Contacts API) — To view and manage contacts
- **Task data** (Tasks API) — To view and manage tasks

We only request access to the data necessary for the specific functionality you are using.

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purposes you authorize, including:

- Performing actions you explicitly request (e.g., creating a document, sending an email, scheduling a calendar event)
- Displaying your information back to you within our applications
- Automating workflows you configure and initiate

**We do not:**

- Sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide the services you request
- Store your data on external servers beyond what is necessary for application functionality
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to the functionality you authorized
- Use your Google user data to develop, improve, or train generalized AI or machine learning models

## Data Storage and Security

- OAuth tokens are stored securely on your local device using system keychain/keyring facilities
- We do not transmit your data to external servers unless required for the specific functionality you are using
- Data processed by our applications is handled in accordance with industry-standard security practices
- You can revoke access to your data at any time through your [Google Account permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)

## Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the services you request. Cached or temporary data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time by removing the application or clearing its data.

## Third-Party Services

Our applications may interact with third-party services (such as Google API Services) on your behalf. These services have their own privacy policies:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data that our applications process
- **Revoke** access to your Google account data at any time via [Google Account settings](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
- **Delete** locally stored application data by removing the application
- **Request information** about how your data is being used

## Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
